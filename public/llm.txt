# GamingSensi — PUBG Mobile & BGMI Sensitivity Configurator

AimSync (GamingSensi) is an AI-powered tactical sensitivity settings generator for PUBG Mobile and BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India). It helps mobile gamers calculate optimal camera, ADS (Aim Down Sights), and gyroscope sensitivity multipliers based on device hardware profiles, screen touch response latencies, finger control claw layouts, and combat roles.

---

## 🛠️ Main Features & Capabilities

- **Interactive Calibration Wizard**: A 7-step onboarding configurator that maps user profiles:
  1. *Hardware Tier*: Flagship, Mid-Range, or Budget/Entry.
  2. *FPS Targets*: 40 FPS, 60 FPS, 90 FPS, or 120 FPS.
  3. *Gyroscope Preferences*: Always-On, Scope-On, or swipe-only (Off).
  4. *Finger Layout*: 2-Finger Thumbs, 3-Finger Claw, 4-Finger Claw, or 5-Finger Claw.
  5. *Combat Role*: Entry Rusher, Mid-Assaulter, Bolt Sniper, or Flex Player.
  6. *Aim Obstacle Correction*: Recoil Control, Close Range Tracking, Horizontal Spray Transfer, Hipfire Reaction Speed, or Long Range Precision.
  7. *Device Spec research*: Automatic AI-based specs calibration (screen Hz, touch sampling rate, sensor analysis) for specified phone models.
- **Dynamic Configuration Profile Dashboard**: Generates precise sensitivity sliders for:
  - *Camera Sensitivity (No Firing)*: No scope, Red Dot/Holo, 2x, 3x, 4x, 6x, 8x.
  - *ADS Sensitivity (Firing recoil)*: Adjusts sliders dynamically based on claw layouts.
  - *Gyroscope Sensitivity*: High-velocity multipliers (up to 400%) with vertical scaling adjustments.
- **Weekly Tactical Training Plans**: Automatically generated routines for recoil control drills.
